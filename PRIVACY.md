# Privacy Policy for Cozy Kitties Health Tracker

**Last Updated:** March 7, 2026

## Overview

Cozy Kitties Health Tracker ("the App") is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

## Data We Access

The App reads the following data from Apple HealthKit:
- **Step Count**: Used to track walking streaks and unlock cats
- **Sleep Analysis**: Used to determine plant growth
- **Environmental Audio Levels**: Used to set weather conditions (optional)

## How We Use Your Data

All data processing happens **entirely on your device**. Specifically:

- Health data is read from HealthKit and processed locally
- Game progress (unlocked cats, plant growth) is stored locally using SwiftData
- No data is ever transmitted to external servers
- No analytics or tracking is implemented
- No advertising is displayed

## Data Storage

- All app data is stored locally on your device
- Data is stored in the app's private sandbox
- Deleting the app removes all stored data

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. Period.

## HealthKit

This app complies with Apple's HealthKit guidelines:
- We only request read access (never write)
- Health data is used solely to provide app functionality
- Health data is never used for advertising or marketing

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.
