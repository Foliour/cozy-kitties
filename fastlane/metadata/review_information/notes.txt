This app uses HealthKit to read step count, sleep analysis, and environmental audio levels. All data is processed entirely on-device and never transmitted to any server.

To test the app:
1. Complete the onboarding flow
2. Grant HealthKit permissions when prompted
3. The apartment view will show your starter cat (Mochi)
4. Walking streaks unlock additional cats (5-day streaks)
5. Quality sleep (7+ hours) helps plants grow

Note: HealthKit data may not be available on the simulator. The app handles this gracefully by showing default states.

No demo account is required - the app works entirely offline with local data.
