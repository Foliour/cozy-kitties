Transform your daily health habits into a cozy apartment filled with adorable cats!

Cozy Kitties turns your Apple Health data into a delightful wellness journey. Walk to grow your cat colony, sleep well to nurture your indoor plants, and enjoy a peaceful environment to brighten your virtual apartment.

KEY FEATURES:

Grow Your Cat Colony
Walk consistently to unlock new cats for your apartment. Each 5-day walking streak brings a new furry friend home. From fluffy Mochi to distinguished Captain, collect all 10 unique cats!

Nurture Indoor Plants
Quality sleep helps your plants flourish. Get 7+ hours of rest to watch your pothos, monstera, and ferns grow from small sprouts to lush greenery.

Dynamic Weather
Your environment affects the weather outside your apartment window. Peaceful surroundings mean sunny days, while louder days bring cozy rain.

SHAME-FREE DESIGN:
No guilt trips. No punishments. If you miss a day, your cats just take longer naps. Your progress is always saved - cats never leave, plants never wilt. This app celebrates your wins without making you feel bad about setbacks.

PRIVACY FIRST:
All your health data stays on your device. We never collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Zero tracking. Zero analytics. Just you and your cozy apartment.

Works with:
- Step count from iPhone or Apple Watch
- Sleep data from Apple Health
- Environmental audio levels (optional)
